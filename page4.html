The Philippines is referred to as Asia's gem of the Orient because of the splendor of its natural surroundings and its opulent culture. Ancient forts, turn-of-the-century houses, museums from today, and cathedrals dating back centuries are all located there. There are 7,100 islands in the Philippine archipelago.


Here are the top 3 destinations in the Philippines:


Siargao

  • Siargao is located in the southern part of Mindanao
  • Also known as the Surfing capital of the Philippines

Boracay

  • Boracay is located in the Western Viasayas region in the PhilippinesL
  • Is well-known for its beaches, which have a lengthy stretch of fine white sand, blue waters, and a breathtaking sunset.

Palawan

  • Located in the Southwsternmost large island here in the Philippines
  • Palawan is famous for the beaches, crystal-clear water,and a limestone cliffs.
